# Trends in Social Expenditure in India

## Introduction 
This Power BI dashboard analyzes India’s social expenditure trends over time, highlighting key indicators such as spending as a percentage of GDP, per capita expenditure, sectoral composition, and state-level distribution. The dashboard provides insights into how social investments have evolved across sectors like education, health, housing, and labor between 2005–2025.

---
## Key Insights 
- Spending as % of GDP: 7.95% ⬇️ (slight decline, indicating fiscal tightening).
- Per Capita Social Expenditure: ₹18,006.76 ⬆️ (growth in welfare spending per citizen).
- Sector-Wise Share: Education (30.54%) and Health (13.88%) dominate the expenditure mix.
- Growth in SE (%): 11.07% ⬇️ (marginal dip compared to previous years).
- Per Capita Capex: ₹2,499.92 ⬆️ (rise in capital spending).
- SE Share in Total Expenditure: 0.56% ⬆️ (improvement in budget prioritization).
- Leading States: Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, and West Bengal are top spenders in 2024–25.
